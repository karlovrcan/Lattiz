About us

Lattiz®, developed by FrieslandCampina, provides restaurants, cafes, hotels, bakeries, and offices an effortless method to produce consistent, high-quality milk foam at the touch of a button.

How it started.

Launched in 2015, Lattiz® is the smart milk solution for coffee professionals, developed over 7 years with baristas, hospitality experts, coffee roasters, and machine manufacturers. Today, thousands of baristas rely on Lattiz® to deliver the perfect milk foam.

Our Vision and Mission

At Lattiz®, our vision is to bring the perfect milk foam to every coffee cup. We aim to make professional milk foam accessible and convenient for all coffee-serving businesses, regardless of their size.

Why Choose Lattiz®?

  • Effortless milk foam production at the touch of a button.
  • Guaranteed consistency with every use.
  • Trusted by professionals in thousands of cafes and restaurants.
  • Backed by FrieslandCampina's years of dairy expertise.
